# Privacy Policy - Webwave Tic Tac Toe

Effective date: February 21, 2026

## Overview

Webwave Tic Tac Toe is a local offline game.  
We do not require account creation and we do not collect personal profile data inside the app.

## Data We Collect

The app stores only basic local game preferences on your device, such as:

- sound on/off
- vibration on/off

This data is stored locally on your phone and is not uploaded to our server.

## Internet Access

The app uses internet access to request and load ads through Google AdMob.  
Gameplay can still run without account login.

## Children

The game is suitable for a general audience.  
If local regulations require age-specific declarations, those must be configured in Google Play Console.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses Google Firebase services for analytics and crash diagnostics:

- Firebase Analytics (app usage events such as screen views and gameplay events)
- Firebase Crashlytics (crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability)

Firebase may process device/app information, diagnostics, crash traces, and usage events as described in Google's policies.

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads.  
AdMob may process data such as device identifiers, ad interaction data, approximate location, and diagnostics for fraud prevention, ad delivery, and performance.

Learn more:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses data in advertising: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
- Firebase data and privacy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Data Deletion

You can clear local app data anytime from Android settings:

`Settings > Apps > Tic Tac Toe > Storage > Clear Data`

For privacy questions, contact: `webwaveglobal@gmail.com`
